BECOME A SPONSOR

If you are interested in becoming a sponsor on any level, please contact our Director of Development, Ally Gold, at sponsors@visionsfilm.org. You can check out our 2015 Sponsorship Package here.

Corporate

Our Corporate Sponsors make it possible for us to keep the festival focused on the art of film and critical studies to contribute to the love of the movie-going experience. We are dedicated to finding the right sponsorship opportunity for you and pride ourselves on the mutually beneficial partnerships that we have developed with sponsors over the years. Please contact our Director of Development to review current sponsorship opportunities or to create your own custom sponsorship. Ask us about custom packages and program advertising.

Donor

Donations in any amount are accepted and greatly appreciated. All donations are tax deductible. Support from donors like you, will subsidize a program that does not generate income, but that is vital to the preservation and growth of our artistic community. Your contribution helps build community, promotes the cinematic arts, and provides creative development opportunities for emerging filmmakers and students. If you are interested in making an in-kind donation, please contact our Director of Development.

In-Kind

Sponsors unable to make a financial contribution can select to become an “In-Kind” sponsor. In-Kind sponsors help to support the festival by donating goods and/or services to the festival or its guests. Like many other festivals, we are looking for gifts and prizes to award our filmmakers. If you are interested in an in-kind donation, please contact our Director of Development.
