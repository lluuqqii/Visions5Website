WHAT IS VISIONS

Visions is an all undergraduate, peer reviewed film festival and conference organized, programmed, sponsored, and hosted by the Film Studies Department and undergraduate film students at the University of North Carolina Wilmington. Every year, the Visions programming committee brings together a diverse and talented group of rising filmmakers and film scholars from around the world. Now in its fifth year, Visions Film Festival & Conference has become a highly attended event full of national and international film screenings, presentations, panels, workshops, and networking opportunities designed for the undergraduate film student.

Year after year, our platform has provided students the opportunity to screen their work to festival audiences, share their ideas, and connect with peers as they prepare to enter a challenging and ever-changing industry. Our goal remains to unite and enrich the international community of undergraduate filmmakers and film scholars by programming fresh, imaginative, and boundary-pushing work.

Join us as we celebrate tomorrow’s visionary filmmakers and film scholars!

The 1 Hour/ 1 Take Video Race

The 1 Hour/1 Take Video Race gives small groups of film enthusiasts a camera and an hour to create a short film and is open to university students as well as the general public. Films that make it to the finish line screen an hour later get to compete for bragging rights and the coveted Audience Choice award. An exciting outreach event inspired by the collaborative spirit of filmmaking, the video race connects our featured undergraduates with the UNCW film community and our festival audience in a creative environment.

Student Conference

Visions is the first undergraduate film festival to honor student work in film studies within the same platform as student film production. Two conference blocks throughout the day feature presentations led by leading undergraduate film scholars. Discussions span a variety of topics, including technology, the avant-garde, and issues of media representations.

Festival Screenings

Visions invites students not only to share their work with a festival audience, but also to discuss it. The festival program is comprised of two screening blocks of undergraduate short films, followed by Q&A panels with the attending filmmakers.
