VISIONS 5 SUBMISSIONS

Please download the "Rules and Terms" and "Submission Process" documents at the bottom of this page. Then, follow the instructions for either a film or presentation abstract submission.

Film submission deadlines

Early: December 5, 2014 ($15 USD)
Regular: January 3, 2015 ($35 USD)
Late: January 27, 2015 ($50 USD)

Films are to be submitted through the Visions5 Film Freeway. All films must be submitted as password protected Vimeo link.

Abstract Submission Deadline

All abstracts are due by January 19, 2015 (no submission fee)

All presentation proposals must be submitted to submissions@visionsfilm.org using the presentation proposal form found at the bottom of this page.

Rules & Guidelines

Films:

  • Film submissions are open to all undergraduate students whose films are completed by an undergraduate key crew (Director, Producer, Cinematographer, Editor, Screenwriter).
  • Films with up to a 25-minute runtime will be accepted. Note: Most programmed films in recent years have had a run time of less than 25 minutes.
  • All films must be in English or have English subtitles. Non-subtitled or dubbed foreign language films will not be considered.
  • Films must be completed between Fall 2012 and January 2015.
  • Film submissions will be accepted exclusively through Film Freeway.
  • For your film to be under consideration, please send proof of your Student Identification Card to submissions@visionsfilm.org.
  • All films must be submitted as a password-protected Vimeo link.
  • By submitting a film, the applicant states that he/she is authorized to submit the film as its Maker or an Agent of the Maker.
  • The applicant agrees that their materials submitted will not be returned.
  • The applicant agrees to allow the Visions Film Festival & Conference to use the applicantâ€™s name and any press materials, including stills of the film, for festival promotion.
  • By submitting a film, the applicant is agreeing to all of the above rules and terms for film submissions.

 

Abstracts:

  • To submit a presentation proposal, please visit our website and email a completed presentation proposal form along with your abstract and author bio to submissions@visionsfilm.org.
  • Presentation proposals are open to all undergraduate students whose abstracts are completed by an undergraduate student.
  • Papers must be completed between Fall 2012 and January 2015. Note: Works-in-progress will not be accepted.
  • All papers must meet MLA guidelines.
  • All presentation proposals must be submitted (via email) using the presentation proposal form found on our website.
  • For your presentation proposal to to considered for programming, please send proof of your Student Identification Card to submissions@visionsfilm.org.
  • By submitting a presentation proposal, the applicant states that he/she is the author of the work, and that any contributor(s) to the work has been cited.
  • The applicant agrees that their materials submitted will not be returned.
  • The applicant agrees to allow the Visions Film Festival & Conference to use the applicant’s name and any press materials, including stills of the film, for festival promotion.
  • By submitting a presentation proposal, the applicant is agreeing to all of the above rules and terms for presentation proposal submissions.

Submission Methods

How To Submit Films

Note: Please be sure to read the rules and terms before submitting your work. All film submissions will be entered via our Film Freeway page. If your film is selected, we will request a copy of your film as an H.264 .mov file. Please send any technical questions to submissions@visionsfilm.org.

  1. Create a Film Freeway account at (https://filmfreeway.com)
  2. Add your project to your account. Please be sure to fill out all of the information that is presented to you during this process.
  3. Click “Save Project.”
  4. Add a digital screener option by entering a password-protected Vimeo link.
  5. Find us on Film Freeway!
  6. Submit your film.
  7. Send an email to submissions@visionsfilm.org with a subject line that reads “Film Submission: Name of Your Film” that addresses the following:
    1. The school in which you are enrolled
    2. Your year of graduation
    3. Please include photo proof of your Student Identification Card.

 

How To Submit Papers

Note: Please be sure to read the rules and terms before submitting your work. If your abstract is selected, we will ask for the complete paper. If your paper is selected, you will be asked to present your work at our event. Please send any questions to submissions@visionsfilm.org.

  1. Visit our website http://www.visionsfilm.org/
  2. Download the Presentation Proposal Form.
  3. Complete the form and save it to your desktop (Note: You must have Adobe Reader XI or a later version to successfully complete and save the form).
  4. Send an email to submissions@visionsfilm.org with the following documents:
    1. Completed Presentation Proposal Form (PDF)
    2. An Abstract (in a Microsoft Word document)
    3. An Author Bio (in a Microsoft Word document)
    4. Photo proof of your Student Identification Card
