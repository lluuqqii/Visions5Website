ONLINE SCORING GUIDELINES

1. Films are listed by entry ID number.

2. Click the link beneath the film's name to go to the online scoring form for that film.

3. On the scoring form, double-check to make sure the film title on the form matches the film you mean to score.

4. Fill out all questions on the form. Include your full name under "Scorer Name."

5. When finished, click "Submit" only ONCE. You will be taken to a confirmation screen.

4. Close the window when finished. Do NOT click "Submit another response" as this will only take you back to the scoring form for the same film.

5. All scores for each week's set of films are to be submitted online no later than Sunday at 9:00PM. All scores are submitted with a timestamp. Scores that are submitted late will be noted and reported immediately to the faculty supervisor.

All scoring forms are confidential. Sharing any information on this page is a direct violation of our Terms & Conditions and is prohibited and monitored.
