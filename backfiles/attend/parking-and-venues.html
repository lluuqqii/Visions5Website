Parking & Venues

PARKING

Parking spaces for visitors are outlined by teal lines and identified by appropriate signs. Visitors may park in designated visitor spaces only.

Visitors with passes may also park in many non-gated parking lots, in either white-lined or yellow-lined spaces, unless the space is otherwise reserved. Parking lots designated as 24-hour faculty/staff parking, reserved spaces and residential parking areas remain off limits.

VISITOR PARKING IN LOT M

UNCW offers visitor parking in parking lot M (located just off Riegel Road and Price Drive). We encourage all visitors to begin their visit at the visitor information booth located at the entrance to Lot M so they can be informed of their parking options and, if necessary, be directed to the preferred parking area by our helpful attendant.

For a map of the UNCW campus and parking, click here.

VISITOR PARKING AT THE DECK

Campus visitors may park at the parking deck located at the corner of Reynolds and Cahill Drives. Visitors may park in designated visitor parking only on the main level.

Parking Deck Rates
First 30 minutes     Free
Hourly Rate     $1.50
Daily Rate     Max. $10 for 24-hour period

VISITOR PARKING PASSES

Visitors who intend to park on campus in areas other than the visitor parking lot, metered spaces or the parking deck will need to purchase a daily or weekly parking pass. All-day parking passes may be purchased for $4 or a weekly parking pass may be purchased for $10.

Visitor parking passes may be obtained in advance at the Parking & Transportation Services Office in the Warwick Center.
