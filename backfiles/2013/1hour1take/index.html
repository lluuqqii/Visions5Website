About the Race

Open to anyone with a passion for film, 1Hour/1Take is an exciting way to connect and collaborate with other film students and film enthusiasts. Participants work in small crews for an hour to create minute-long videos shot in one take—each one competing for the coveted Audience Choice Award.

At the end of the day, not only will you walk away with a new network of filmmakers, but also with a festival-screened video. Now that's a win-win situation.

Registration Is Free

There are currently no fees to participate in the video race. Simply fill out the registration form when registration opens. Stay tuned for updates on our website or on Facebook.



Congratulations to the Red Team, winners of the 2012 1HR/1TK Video Race Audience Choice Award! And a job well done to all.

Any questions? Shoot an email to Video Race Coordinator Marge Streb at events@visionsfilm.org.
