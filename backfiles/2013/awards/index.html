Awards are issued during the After Party in recognition of individuals’ achievements in filmmaking and scholarship.  The categories include: Excellence in Animation, Excellence in Documentary, Excellence in Experimental, Excellence in Narrative, Excellence in Scholarship, Audience Choice in Filmmaking, and Audience Choice in Scholarship.  The prime award of the festival, the Visionary Award, is presented to the filmmaker who expresses the most unique and creative vision.
