Visions is an all-undergraduate, peer-reviewed film festival and conference organized, programmed, and hosted by undergraduate film students at the University of North Carolina Wilmington. Every year, the Visions programming committee brings together a diverse and talented group of rising filmmakers and film scholars from other undergraduate film programs around the world.

Now in our third year since opening our submissions to international students, Visions has become a highly-attended event full of international film screenings and student presentations, panels, workshops, and networking opportunities designed for the undergraduate film student.

FESTIVAL SCREENINGS

Our burgeoning young artists are invited not only to share their work with a festival audience, but to also discuss it. The festival program is comprised of two screening blocks of undergraduate short films, followed by Q&A panels with the attending filmmakers.

STUDENT CONFERENCE

Visions is the first undergraduate film festival to honor student work in film studies within the same platform as student film production. Two conference blocks throughout the day feature presentations led by leading undergraduate film scholars. From Hollywood to Bollywood, these discussions span a variety of topics, including technology, the avant-garde, and issues of media representations.

THE 1 HOUR / 1 TAKE VIDEO RACE

Open to university students and the general public, the 1 Hour / 1Take Video Race gives small groups of film enthusiasts a camera and an hour to create a short film. Films that make it to the finish line screen an hour later to compete for bragging rights and the coveted Audience Choice award. An exciting outreach event inspired by the collaborative spirt of filmmaking, the video race connects our featured undergraduates with the UNCW film community and our festival audience in a creative environment.


Year after year, our platform has provided students the opportunity to screen their work to festival audiences, share their ideas, and connect with peers as they prepare to enter a challenging and ever-changing industry. Our goal remains to unite and enrich the international community of undergraduate filmmakers and film scholars by programming fresh, imaginative, and boundary-pushing work.

Join us as we celebrate tomorrow’s filmmakers and film scholars!
