Join the Tour

By partnering with other universities and programs throughout the year to share films and presentations featured at the Festival & Conference, the Visions Tour provides an enriching program geared towards students of all disciplines. We visit schools throughout the Southeastern United States during fall and spring semesters to screen international undergraduate films and encourage film and paper submissions.

Even some of the best undergraduate work won't be seen outside of the festival and conference circuit. As part of our mission to unite the international undergraduate film community and bridge the gap between film production and film studies, Visions Film hopes to share the best undergraduate films and papers we receive each year with young filmmakers and film scholars at our partnering universities.

Interested in having your university's program or department become a stop on the Visions Tour? Contact Festival Director Shannon Silva at silvas@uncw.edu.
