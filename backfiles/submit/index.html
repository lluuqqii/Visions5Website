Submissions Overview

The Visions Film Festival & Conference, presented by the Film Studies Department at the University of North Carolina Wilmington, has opened submissions for the 3rd Annual Visions Film Festival & Conference, to be held April 5, 2013.

The Festival welcomes submissions of undergraduate short films in narrative, documentary, experimental, and animation categories, as well as undergraduate academic papers.

The remaining deadlines to submit U.S. and international films and paper abstracts for the 2013 Visions Film Festival & Conference are:

January 7, 2013 (arrive by)
Normal Deadline for Films; Abstracts Deadline for Papers

• Note: this is the last deadline for submitting abstracts for papers to be considered for the April 5th festival and conference. If selected in the preliminary screening, a final paper will be requested to continue the review process.

February 15, 2013 (arrive by)
Late Deadline for Films Only

Questions regarding film submissions? Email submissions@visionsfilm.org.
