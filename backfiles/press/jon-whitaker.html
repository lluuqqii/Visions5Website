Marketing Plans/Proposals
2013 PR Campaign Plan
In this proposal, I outlined key objectives and positionings for this year's publicity campaign in regards to strengthening the Festival's image and brand as an undergraduate resource and a leading undergraduate festival. I established five goals for my PR team, geared toward improving our online presence among our target audiences, as well as establishing stronger relationships with sponsors to create a stronger sense of endorsement for the Festival. Some new tools I plan to implement in this year's campaign in terms of increasing audience engagement include social payment initiatives, animated posters, and improved social media and video coverage of the event.

Sponsorship Proposal (w/ Letter)
I created this proposal as part of our overall objective of establishing stronger sponsor partnerships. Previous festival years did not have a sponsorship proposal to offer potential partners. Within this packet, I outline to sponsors the uniqueness of the Visions Film Festival and it's expected areas of growth. I also outline customizable opportunities for brand integration, sponsor attendance, and visibility.
